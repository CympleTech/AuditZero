Auditable zkPayments Across Chains

Seamless, privacy-preserving payments with optional auditability. Built on ZK proofs, supporting both secp256k1 and ed25519 signature systems.

Why Private Yet Auditable Payments Matter

The Problem

  • Traditional payments lack privacy protection
  • Existing privacy solutions aren't auditable
  • Cross-chain transfers are complex and expensive
  • Regulatory compliance is challenging with privacy

Our Solution

  • Zero-knowledge proofs ensure transaction privacy
  • Dual-memo system enables selective auditability
  • Native cross-chain support with unified interface
  • Compliance-ready for institutional adoption
